## Notes
Product Hunt launch creates multiple backlinks via alternatives pages, product listings, and community discussion. Also drives direct signups and social proof.

Time this after Reddit authority campaigns are established and initial outreach has landed.

## Checklist
- [ ] Prepare PH listing assets (logo, tagline, gallery images, maker video)
- [ ] Write compelling first comment / maker story
- [ ] Line up hunter (if using external hunter)
- [ ] Coordinate launch day social pushes (LinkedIn, Reddit, email)
- [ ] Prepare for day-of engagement (reply to every comment)
- [ ] Schedule for Tuesday-Thursday launch day
