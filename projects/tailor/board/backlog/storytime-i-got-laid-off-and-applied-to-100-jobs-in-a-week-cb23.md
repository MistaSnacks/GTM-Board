## Concept
Personal story format. Someone who lost their job (DOGE cuts, tech layoffs, etc.) and used TAILOR to mass-apply efficiently. Focus on the emotional journey, not the product.

## Hook
"I got laid off 3 weeks ago. Here's how I applied to 100 jobs in 7 days."

## Key Beats
1. The layoff moment (emotional, relatable)
2. The overwhelm of rewriting resumes for each application
3. Finding TAILOR — "I just paste the job description and it does the rest"
4. The results: interviews started coming in
5. Soft CTA: "If you're in the same boat, I'll leave the link"

## Format
- 60-90 seconds
- Face cam, talking head, casual setting
- Raw and authentic — NOT polished
- Can be creator's real story or scripted testimonial

## Why This Works
"DOGE Federal" and "600K Cuts" ads resonated in Meta. The layoff angle is timely and emotional. Storytime format gets highest completion rates on TikTok.
